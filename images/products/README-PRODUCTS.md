## Image Specifications

### **Format:**
- JPG or PNG
- High resolution (2000x2000 pixels minimum)
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)

### **Quality:**
- 4K-ready (2000x2000 to 4000x4000)
- High quality (90% JPG compression)
- Clean white or neutral background
- Professional product photography

### **File Size:**
- Target: 200-500KB per image
- Compress with TinyJPG.com

---

## Photo Types Needed

### **1. Main Image (-main.jpg)**
- Front-facing product shot
- Clean white background
- Product centered
- Highest quality
- This shows on product cards

### **2. Angle 2 (-2.jpg)**
- Side or 3/4 view
- Same background
- Shows different angle

### **3. Angle 3 (-3.jpg)**
- Back or top view
- Shows product details
- Label visible

### **4. Lifestyle/Detail (-4.jpg)**
- Product in use or styled setting
- Can show texture/details
- Lifestyle context

## Image Optimization

Before uploading:

1. **Resize** to 2000x2000 pixels (use Photopea.com - free)
2. **Compress** with TinyJPG.com (reduces 50-70%)
3. **Rename** to exact file names above
4. **Upload** to `images/products/` folder
